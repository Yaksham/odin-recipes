Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs is a fluffy and delicious egg dish made by gently stirring eggs over a pan, optionally mixed with cream or milk. You can have them as is or season with salt and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 thin slices cooked ham, diced (Optional)
  • 2 tablespoons sliced Cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon heavy whipping cream
  • Â½ teaspoon butter

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs in a small bowl until smooth. Mix in ham, Cheddar cheese, and heavy cream.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture; cook and stir until set but still moist, 3 to 5 minutes.
